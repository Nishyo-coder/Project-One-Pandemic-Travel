    Covid-19 Traveling Tips

  • Check local requirements, restrictions and situations
  • Make paper copies of test results and screening documentation
  • Plan for long hours in masks: With layovers, long flights, and ground transportation, you may find you and your family in a mask long hours. You want to pack extra masks!
  • Pack Snacks! Some airport restaurants are not fully open or close early. Check desired location's guidance and restrictions.
  • For Covid Safety Tips, check out: https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/coronavirus/in-depth/coronavirus-safe-travel-advice/art-20486965
